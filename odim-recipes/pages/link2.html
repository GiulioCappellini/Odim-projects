Ramen

A Japanese noodle soup served in a flavorful broth, usually with meat, eggs, and vegetables. It’s warm, comforting, and highly customizable.

Ingredients

  • 2 packs ramen noodles
  • 1 liter chicken broth
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1/2 teaspoon grated ginger
  • 2 eggs
  • 100g sliced chicken or pork
  • 1/2 cup chopped green onions

Directions

  1. Bring water to boil and cook eggs (6–7 minutes for soft-boiled). Set aside.
  2. In another pot, heat sesame oil (1 minute).
  3. Add garlic and ginger, cook (1–2 minutes).
  4. Pour in chicken broth and soy sauce, simmer (5–10 minutes).
  5. Add sliced meat and cook (5 minutes).
  6. Cook ramen noodles separately (3–4 minutes).
  7. Assemble: noodles in bowl → broth → meat → halved eggs → green onions.
