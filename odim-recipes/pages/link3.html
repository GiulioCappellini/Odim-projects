Pudding

A smooth and creamy dessert topped with caramel sauce. It has a soft texture and a sweet, slightly burnt sugar flavor.

Ingredients

Caramel

  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup water

Pudding

  • 2 cups milk
  • 3 eggs
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions

  1. In a pan, melt sugar with water over medium heat until golden (8–10 minutes).
  2. Pour caramel into a mold and spread evenly.
  3. In a bowl, mix milk, eggs, sugar, and vanilla.
  4. Pour mixture into the caramel mold.
  5. Place mold in a water bath (bain-marie).
  6. Bake at 180°C (350°F) for 45–50 minutes.
  7. Let it cool, then refrigerate for at least 2 hours.
  8. Unmold before serving.
