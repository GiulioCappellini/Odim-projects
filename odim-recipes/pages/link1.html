Lasagna

A classic baked pasta dish made with layers of pasta sheets, meat sauce, and melted cheese. It’s rich, hearty, and very filling.

Ingredients

  • 9 lasagna sheets
  • 400g ground beef
  • 2 cups tomato sauce
  • 1 small onion (chopped)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat (2–3 minutes).
  3. Add onion and garlic, cook until soft (3–5 minutes).
  4. Add ground beef, cook until browned (7–10 minutes).
  5. Pour in tomato sauce, season with salt and pepper, and simmer (10 minutes).
  6. In a baking dish, layer: sauce → lasagna sheets → cheese. Repeat layers.
  7. Finish with mozzarella and parmesan on top.
  8. Bake for 30–35 minutes.
  9. Let it rest for 10 minutes before serving.
